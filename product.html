Rubber Paint

Rubber Liquid is a versatile material with exceptional properties like flexibility, durability, and resistance to weathering, is used in various industries for coating surfaces, sealing leaks, and waterproofing.
Rubber Seal is crucial in mechanical and structural applications, providing a secure barrier against fluids, gases, or debris. Made from durable materials like natural rubber or synthetic compounds which prevent leaks.

Prime

Plaster prime is a water-based primer used in construction and renovation projects to prepare surfaces for plastering. It promotes adhesion, reduces moisture absorption, and provides uniform surfaces for even plaster finish.
Iron prime a primer or coating designed for iron surfaces, containing rust inhibitors to prevent corrosion and rust formation. It provides a protective layer, extending the lifespan of iron materials in various applications like outdoor structures, industrial machinery, automotive components, and household appliances.

Sheen

Low Sheen it provide a smooth, soft appearance with a slight reflective quality. They are popular for interior walls and exterior surfaces, offering a durable, weather-resistant coating that resists moisture and UV damage.
Sheen Stain ia wood stain that provides a subtle, lustrous finish to wooden surfaces, enhancing their natural beauty and providing protection. Available in various glossiness levels, sheen stains are commonly used in woodworking and furniture.

Gamazine & Granite

Gamazine is a luxurious decorative coating made from marble dust, quartz, and polymers. It provides a durable, waterproof layer, making it popular in interior design for walls and ceilings. Customizable in colors and finishes.
Granite is coating that mimics the natural appearance of granite on surfaces. It consists of paint pigments, additives, and textured materials, creating a multi-dimensional finish. It enhances the aesthetics of homes, offices, and commercial spaces.
